PAWSTER CAFE

Cafe

Pawster provides a multi-cuisine restaurant for you and your pooches. With spacious indoor and out door seating, we provide an enjoyable experience where you can enjoy your food while playing with your pet.

Our dog food menu in one of the best in India, specially curated by Rashee Kuchroo – a renowned pet dietician, from Doggie Dabbas, as we want all the pooches to eat nutritious and healthy food.

Come over with your pet to have a memorable time at our café!
