about pawster

We’re a young organisation with a vision to make pet ownership hassle free. We provide different services to make the lives of pet owners and pet lovers seamless. Our offerings include Dog Crèche, Pet Therapy, Dog Café, Dog Training, and Animal Sensitization events, along with promoting adoption to ensure all our furry friends have a home!

We have an ecosystem of pet owners, pet lovers, behaviourists and Dog trainers who enable each other to grow efficiently and effectively. We cater to those in need with our services. Our focus on working with international quality standards has been instrumental in gathering a loyal and satisfied customer base, helping make us the fastest growing brand in Pet space in India!
